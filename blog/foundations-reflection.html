How much I grow when I am vulnerable

At the start of this course, I promised myself that THIS, new, journey I was embarking on, was to better myself in every aspect. I feel I have done this, or at least, I think I have. My physical body is not in amazing shape. Lockdown sure does make me eat. But, I love it and I love myself.

Being able to express my own doubts has been a struggle for me. I was always intimidated when I felt looked down on but the core reflections allowed me so not feel singled out. It kept me embracing that I am part of something bigger than my own worries.

Wait... My brain can change?

Neuroplasticity has saved my life. Growth mindset has saved my life. Being able to feel like I am not a lost cause has saved my life. I am so thankful for the lessons on these topics. I have always thought that I could only have a finite amount of knowledge. How wrong I was.

Even if I am not able to understand everything I learn or are able to use it. I am not scared or intimidated because I don't know it, YET, but I will know it soon. Having that amount of confidence in my abilities has shaped me to be more accepting of challenges and obstacles that occur.

I can actually stick to a plan?!
